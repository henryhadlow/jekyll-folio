Lasting Power of Attorney — radically simplifying one of the most complex legal processes

Lasting Power of Attorney is a way to appoint someone you trust to manage your money, property and healthcare, in case anything happens to you.

The previous process involved fourteen separate forms and guidance documents totaling over 200 pages.

We completely rethought the whole process. Now there are three forms (one is optional) and just one guidance document.

We rewrote all the text making it simpler and clearer, and removing as much legal jargon as possible.

The new forms are currently part of a government consultation. If successful, they will launch in 2014.
